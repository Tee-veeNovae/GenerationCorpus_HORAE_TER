<https://boilerpipe-web.appspot.com/bible,14O-16-3,2-chroniques.php> Il

<https://boilerpipe-web.appspot.com/bible,14O-16-4,2-chroniques.php> Ben

<https://boilerpipe-web.appspot.com/bible,14O-16-5,2-chroniques.php>

<https://boilerpipe-web.appspot.com/bible,14O-16-6,2-chroniques.php> Le

<https://boilerpipe-web.appspot.com/bible,14O-16-7,2-chroniques.php>

pas appuyé sur l'Éternel, ton Dieu, l'armée du roi de Syrie s'est
échappée de tes mains.

<https://boilerpipe-web.appspot.com/bible,14O-16-8,2-chroniques.php> Les

<https://boilerpipe-web.appspot.com/bible,14O-16-9,2-chroniques.php> Car

<https://boilerpipe-web.appspot.com/bible,14O-16-10,2-chroniques.php>

<https://boilerpipe-web.appspot.com/bible,14O-16-11,2-chroniques.php>
